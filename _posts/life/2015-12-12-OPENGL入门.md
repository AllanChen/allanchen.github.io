### 创建渲染缓冲区
<pre class="prettyprint">
GLuint m_renderbuffer;//创建一个渲染缓冲区对象
<pre>
<pre class="prettyprint">
glGenRenderbuffers(1, &m_renderbuffer);//创建一个渲染缓冲区对象
<pre class="prettyprint">
<pre class="prettyprint">
glBindRenderbuffer(GL_RENDERBUFFER, m_renderbuffer);//将该渲染缓冲区对象绑定到管线上
